The day is here! We're so excited to share Beyond (beta) with a small group of beta users today. If you just got an email from us, then you're part of a group of humans who we're inviting to be our exclusive first set of users. You get to try it first! 🎉

Beyond is a platform designed to help you take control of your content consumption online. There's so much stuff out there, and at times it can be hard to make sense of it all. We want to help you make sense of it all.

That's a big task! So today we're happy to share with you the first release, which is designed to help you save, organise, and manage your own favourite things from the Internet. You'll be able to easily capture links, organise them with tags, and take notes.

You can also add them to your public profile, so you can share your favourite stuff with friends, too.

![alt text](https://cdn.gobeyond.so/misc/blog/i1.png "Beyond Roadmap")

After we've helped you take back control of your own favourite things, we're going to focus on making it a breeze to share the best things with friends, so that you can learn from each other.

This paves the way for us to turn Beyond into your go-to place for you to discover the best content. We'll improve discoverability, and and redefine how we interact with each other around content.

**Beyond is your new homebase for content. Welcome!**
